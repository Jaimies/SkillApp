Privacy Policy

Starting with version 4.0.0, SkillApp does not collect any of your data. The application does not even have the INTERNET permission, meaning it cannot connect to the Internet.

For version 3.5.0 and earlier see the old Privacy Policy.
Starting with version 4.0.0, you can find the app version in the settings; if the version is not shown, you must be on an older version and thus covered by the old Privacy Policy.
